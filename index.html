    How to Play Moviele

    • You have 6 guesses
    • Green in any column indicates a match!
    • Yellow in Release Date column indicates 5 years within the movie's release date.
    • Yellow in Genres column indicates one of the same genres as the movie's.
    • Yellow in Lead Actor column indicates the first letter of the first or last name is the same as the movie's lead actor.
    • Yellow in Director column indicates the first letter of the first or last name is the same as the movie's director.
    • Yellow in Rating column indicates 0.5 within the movie's rating out of 10.
    • There is a movie daily at 12 AM EST.
